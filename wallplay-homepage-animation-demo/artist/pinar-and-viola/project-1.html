Pinar & Viola

x Adidas

January, 2016 Herzogenaurach, Germany

Pinar & Viola were asked by Adidas HQ to rejuvenate their image archive.

The artist duo digitally handcrafted contemporary sports posters where Adidas athletes defy the realities of physical realm, floating genderless together on a groundless space, beyond the laws of gravity and time.


Adidas uses these images as internally for visual inspiration
