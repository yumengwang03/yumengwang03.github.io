Pinar & Viola

x IKEA

June, 2017

IKEA commissioned Pinar & Viola to create the The STUNSIG print collection under their name. Their work has been displayed onto a variety of IKEA products, as a part of their limited print collection.



IKEA asked Pınar & Viola to create prints that would attract, inspire, and surprise people all over the world. They wanted the duo to create art pieces that would go on everyday objects, such as bed linen sets, cushions, carpets, trays, boxes, t-shirts, bags, and porcelain.

In response to IKEA’s request, Pinar & Viola created a collection which invites the consumer to embrace the strange, the unknown, and the alien.
