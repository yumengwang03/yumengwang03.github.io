Under the alias of KidSuper, Colm Dillane prints his sketches onto clothing, creates multi-media lookbooks, directs music videos, produces claymation videos, runs a recording studio, operates a clothing store & has developed an artist residency program—all under one roof.

KidSuper is an idea created from the desire to dream and rediscover the wonderment of being a kid. KidSuper came from those childhood memories of when you ran around in your underwear with a homemade cape, convinced that you had superpowers and nothing and no one could tell you otherwise. KidSuper works to recapture this magic, and to re-explore the kid in all of us that is screaming for adventure, creativity, and sheer enjoyment. KidSuper’s mission is to remind people that anything is possible.

Colm Dillane was born in NYC in 1991, he is the multi-media artist & entertainer behind KidSuper, a hybrid art brand with a storefront and studio in Williamsburg. The only child of two world travelers, Dillane moved often as a small child and attended public high school in Brooklyn--a wide range of life experience fueled Colm’s super hero imagination & need to create his own world through art & design.

A true renaissance man, Dillane graduated with a degree in Math from NYU and played professional soccer in Brazil for a year between highschool and college.
