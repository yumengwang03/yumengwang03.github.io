Olivia Steele

The Basement at EDITION Hotel, Miami Beach

Art Basel Miami Beach, 2015-Present

For Art Basel Miami Beach, 2015/2016 The EDITION Hotel in Miami Beach featured three permanent installations in the hotel's nightclub, bowling alley, and ice rink, forming The Basement.

For the 2015 installation and reception, the hotel also collaborated with Soto Sake, printing over 200 books to gift to guests who attended the cocktail evening. Olivia currently has three neon works on permanent display in The Basement, including Future Memories, I Had Too Much To Dream Last Night, and What If This Is All Real?
