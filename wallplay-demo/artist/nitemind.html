Nitemind

Nitemind uses light as their medium to create mesmeric visual experiences of motion, light, and color.

Nitemind creates customized software and LED sculptures to induce mesmeric visual experiences of motion, light, and color. Nitemind combines technology and physical objects to create immersive and interactive installations. Their works have featured laser beams, projection-mapping, and software art installations in combination with ephemeral, site-specific performances.

Their work has appeared through a wide range of media and genres including light art, algorithmic design, livestreaming, projection mapping, environmental design, optimized design, interactive experiences, music supervision, art direction, stage design, and virtual reality.

Led by Michael Roddy Potvin, Nitemind Creative is a group of forward-thinking artists, engineers, designers, and curators who believe in a holistic approach to their work. They seek to impart mindfulness, enthusiasm, and an art-centered approach to every inch of what we do. Highly engaged in the NYC event and online community, Nitemind is uniquely hyper aware of what is happening in both the underground arts and corporate world.

Nitemind has been instrumental in the development of exhibitions at the Palais de Tokyo, Metropolitan Museum of Art, MoMA, Johannes Vogt Gallery, Red Bull Music Academy, and many others.
