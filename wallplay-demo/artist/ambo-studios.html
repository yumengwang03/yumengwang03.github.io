ambo studios creates immersive experiences through fabricated structures, projections, and interactive systems. They utilize interactive, user driven systems to control video, 3D environments and experiential interfaces. Through fabricated structures, projected mapped surfaces, physical controllers, sensors, and much more users can experience and influence each installation individually.

The ambo studios duo combines physical experiences with the latest technology to bring people together to share a collective moment of inspiration.

ambo studios is Conor Oberlander & Ben Grace. Grace and Oberlander are passionate about the power of shared human experience. Their goal is to bring people together to share a collective moment of inspiration and wonder.

ambo studios forges inspiring, engaging systems - through a combined knowledge of Industrial Design, Architecture, Fabrication, Motion, Sound Design, Light and System Interactivity.

Oberlander & Grace have worked with brands, festivals, musicians and fashion labels.
