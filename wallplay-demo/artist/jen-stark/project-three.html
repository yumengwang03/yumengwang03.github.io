Jen Stark

x Miami International Airport

International, Ongoing

Artist Jen Stark has created Meltdown, a permanent installation inside the Miami International Airport. You can find the between Terminal D&E on the 3rd level, just off the moving skywalk.


“For this Miami International Airport commission I created Meltdown, a large scale mural depicting organic patterns oozing down, creating a waterfall of colors.”- Jen Stark
