RJ Raizk

RJ Raizk creates surreal hand drawn patterns, paintings and digital work in a variety of media.

As an artist in NYC, RJ Raizk draws a lot of his inspiration from the streets and organic patterns, such as the cellular structure of plans and the cosmos. Stylistically he gravitates towards the psychedelic and surreal; anything that can remove people from their everyday lives. He tends to create in waves and whatever looks good to him at the time is what he moves forward with.

Originally from Ohio, RJ Raizk moved to NYC to attend The School of Visual Arts (SVA). While in school, Raizk realized his geometric and dynamic style developed out of doodling and inspirations drawn from the surreal, organic, cosmic, and psychedelic. Since then, he has amassed an impressive list of commissions, including MTV, Restoration Hardware, Intermix, NYC nightclub Up&Down, and Tribeca Grand Hotel, among others.

“A glance through the artist’s portfolio, which he endearingly refers to as his ‘pattern book’, reveals that RJ’s work is delicate and energetic at the same time. Patterns that look like constellations, electrons, cell mitosis, photosynthesis or seed-germination all make an appearance in optic black and white ink on paper, all created entirely by hand.
