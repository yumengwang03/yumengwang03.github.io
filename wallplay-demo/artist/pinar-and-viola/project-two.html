Pinar & Viola

Healing Prints

Paris, 2016

Pinar & Viola’s studio launches a new collection each year, to showcase the “near-future” of images in accordance with their artist statement.



In 2016, Pinar & Viola digitally crafted the collection Healing Prints, comprised of videos, garments, and prints that are meant to revitalise and celebrate the sacredness of nature.

Healing Prints is described by the artists as a collection of rebellious visuals fabricated into unique fashion statements.
