FKA Twigs: LP1

Album Release

Experiential Installation

   featuring:  Art by Jesse Kanda | Pop Up Record Shop | Multimedia installation at wallplay

Wallplay hosted a multimedia exhibition for the FKA Twigs LP1 album launch, featuring art by Jesse Kanda.

The public art billboards and lightboxes at 118 Orchard Street featured Twigs’ iconic LP1 album cover and served as a back-drop for fans and passersby to take photographs. Deluxe editions of LP1 were sold inside where Twigs' video collaboration with Kanda played on 12 digital screens.

What we did: venue, gallery partnership, art billboard production, launch event, support for album launch campaign. The exhibition garnered 3.5mm physical views and was extended from its original run time by fan requests.
