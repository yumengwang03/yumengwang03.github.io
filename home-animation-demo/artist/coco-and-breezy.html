Coco and Breezy Eyewear is a NYC based eyewear company founded in 2009 by twin designers Corianna and Brianna Dotson. They believe in creating high quality artistic designs that are functional and fashionable. With their attention to detail, they have developed their own unique style through their signature cut-outs and architectural frame shapes, inspired by Art Deco.

"Our Eyewear is the Outfit ... and Your Clothing is the Accessory" - Coco and Breezy

Coco and Breezy was founded in 2009 by twin designers Corianna and Brianna Dotson. Their early designs, each handmade by the twins, were an instant hit in New York, and were found on major celebrities and entertainers within weeks of the brand's launch. Their eyewear has braced celebrity faces such as Prince, Lady Gaga, Beyonce, Kelly Osbourne, Nicki Minaj and more.

Over time, Coco and Breezy have refined their design and production process, engaging in increasingly ambitious design projects, ranging from designing "third-eye" sunglasses for the late Prince, working with numerous artists to define their style, and with brands as divergent as Hershey's and Foot Locker's Six:02 athleisure concept store. The designers have been featured in most major industry publications, including Vogue and Harper's Bazaar.

Music and creativity are integral to their design practice. The twins DJ and create visual art in their spare time.
