Pinar & Viola are inspired by both the beauty and pitfalls of contemporary culture, namely digital obsession. The artist duo busy themselves with digital image making to ironically escape the excessive proliferation of images. Ultimately, their handcrafted work is comprised of qualitative, ethical, and future-oriented visual imagery as commentary on what they view as a quantitative, unethical world.

For each project, both artists begin by partaking in extensive research on the present subject of their choice. As an example, Each artist will excessively research historical folklore to better pinpoint patterns of folkloric expression in the digital age, ultimately inspiring their original designs.

Pinar & Viola strive to make digital design feel sustainable and valuable through balancing isolating technology with community and nature. Experimenting with any and all media that can create a visual identity and elevated image, Pinar & Viola strive to work with design systems that they feel contain a natural and practical edge. "The sculptor, a carpenter of our time, has become the 3D printer of today,” they’ve exclaimed. Pinar & Viola find ways to expose these connections to the public, such as through “couture digital,” where their visual work is created through meticulous digital painting and selected to be printed onto fashion-forward wearables, associated with the traditionally tangible and handcrafted.

Pinar Demirdag (Montreal, CA 1985- ) and Viola Renate (Ede, NL 1982- ) met during their graduate studies at the Sandberg Instituut, Amsterdam.


Since 2009, they have been collaborating to create handcrafted, original design as Pinar & Viola. The duo are currently based in Paris, France with their headquarters remaining in Amsterdam, Netherlands. Inventing a profession of the future coined “Image Couture,” the European artist duo work with brands and industries who are the leaders in their line of expertise. Pinar & Viola have also been generously supported by The Dutch Government for their entire collaborative career.
