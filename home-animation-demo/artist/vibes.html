Selffee uses their technology and app to make edible selfies on the spot.

Take a photo using their smartphone and app, they will print that image using their exclusive technology (that uses FDA certified edible inks), directly onto iced coffee, iced tea, and cookies. No sugar paper involved.

Selffee is always testing on new foodstuffs, so they can bring that extra creativity to your next event to immerse your guests with an innovative experience.

Their live experience has appeared through a wide range of events from brand activations, pop-ups, private parties, art openings and more.

Farsh and David met at a silent meditation retreat in Northern Thailand. Once it finished they haven’t stopped talking….

Farsh Kanji​ ­ Co-Founder - Spent the last 11 years in health and wellness as a clinical director and practicing chiropractor. Dr.Farsh built and then sold his multidisciplinary wellness center to move east. He helped uplift his surrounding community and coached them to healthier living. As a side project he ran a mens outerwear start-up and also an events business concurrently. Farsh gave back by volunteering as a chiropractor a few times overseas. People describe him as having an entrepreneurial mind, a philanthropic heart and a people­-first value orientation. With an artistic eye he incorporates visual sensibilities into his growing portfolio of work.

David Weiss​ Co-Founder - After 6 years of directing a sales team in a top New York fashion brand he set his sights on horticulture. After successfully building and then selling a commercial greenhouse business specializing in tomatoes David returned to New York City to bridge the gap between experiential food and fun with Selffee. He has demonstrated a proven track record in launching new products and services and continues to follow his passion of spreading joy through innovation in the food industry.
