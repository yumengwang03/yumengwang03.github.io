Love bErto is a self taught artist working through a variety of media including acrylics, oils, wood etchings, and spray paint.

A bi-coastal nomad based between New York and Los Angeles, his artistic expression thrives on cultural diversity and the intensity of city life but also in peaceful communion with nature through the art of surfing. This fusion is the love affair that inspires the body of work known as Love bErto.

Love bErto is based between Brooklyn, New York and Venice Beach, California. Originally a street muralist, his work has evolved to canvas and printmaking. As an artist of few words, he's a living nomad, growing his body of work from the experiences he encounters around the globe.

His work continues with a focus on melding abstract cultural identities with the softer tones of Mother Nature in a harmonious way.
