Gustavo Torres is an Argentinian motion designer, art director, illustrator, and painter. He is currently based in Buenos Aires. Working online has allowed him to acquire a large list of clients worldwide.

Torres' work as Kidmograph is a mix between the digital and analog, evoking historical representations of the future through cyberpunk and retro aesthetics.

Kidmograph's work has been featured on numerous websites including The Creators Project, We the Urban, Instagram, and Art Basel. His client list has ranged from independent musicians to large companies, including Twitter, Paramount Pictures, Adidas, and Toyota.

Torres has an extensive career as a music video producer, having worked with Kanye West, John Legend, Travi$, and Nas. His work in the music video industry opened a door to producing live visual projects. As a result, he has worked closely with several international DJs. He was A-Trak's art director from 2013 to 2015.

Since 2014, Torres has been a part of Tumblr Creatrs, a division of Tumblr creatives that connects artists with brands. Since 2016, he's also been a Senior Art Director at Instagram.
