Working across fine arts and commercial design, Wiena Lin strives to create stunning sensory experiences in the physical world by extending the expressiveness of traditional art and design media with emerging technology.

Lin relentlessly pursues new tools and partnerships to communicate ideas in the most impactful medium for each particular message. Her approach is deeply rooted in marketing strategies that convert a creative seed into a measurable reality.

Wiena Lin is a multi-disciplinary artist and designer based out of Brooklyn, New York. Her most recent projects include design/strategy for AR, an experiential installation commissioned by the Smithsonian, and a functional system of networked wearables.
