Alina Landry lives and works in Brooklyn, New York. Her work expands over a wide range of disciplines. Landry has conceptualized video and animation projects, produced, directed, and edited both fiction and documentary short films, designed and animating titles, created broadcast branding, composited visual effects, storyboarding, and style frames for commercials, and provided art direction and styling for photo and video shoots.

Alina Landry Rancier is an interdisciplinary artist currently based in Brooklyn, NY. Alina has over 11 years of experience art directing, creating motion graphics, directing, and working in post-production for commercials, short films, photoshoots, and music videos.

Within this time she has had the pleasure of working with several of the best companies and brands in the world including Google, HP, ASUS, Nike, Converse, PBS, AMEX, and Mastercard.
