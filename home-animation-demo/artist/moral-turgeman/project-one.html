Moral Turgeman

The Little House

Traveling Installation

This traveling mirrored house explores the idea that home is something that can be reflected anywhere. Explore its inner depths to experience a vessel of ascension. The enclosed circular room is designed to aid the rise of frequency and equipped with 8 stations that emit binaural sound waves.

   The Little House was created to boost the connection that we have with our inner and outer selves.
